taj mahal

Taj Mahal has been regarded as the symbol of Shah Jahan's love for his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.Located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh.

Khajuraho Temple

Khajuraho Temples are a group of Hindu and Jain temples in Madhya Pradesh, India, renowned for their Nagara-style architecture and intricate sculptures. .

Sun Temple, Konark

The Sun Temple in Konark, India, is an impressive structure built in the 13th century during King Narasingha Deva I's reign.

Gomateshwara Statue

The Gomateshwara statue, also known as Bahubali, is a 57-foot (17 m) high monolithic statue located on Vindhyagiri Hill, or Indra-giri, in Shravanbelagola.

Hampi

Hampi is a village and temple town located on the banks of the Tungabhadra River in the state of Karnataka, India..

The Golden Temple In Punjab

The Golden Temple has a rich history and has been a focal point for various movements and conflicts, including the Singh Sabha Movement and the Punjabi Suba movement.

The Nalanda University

Nalanda University is known for its eco-friendly initiatives, such as becoming a plastic-free campus and implementing a 'Net Zero' eco-recycling strategy.

  • Entry :₹30 (Indians); ₹500 (foreigners)
  • Best time to visit:October–March, daily 6 a.m.–6 p.m.
  • Tour packages: 3‑night guided cultural trip ₹12,000–₹18,000 pp (accommodation, tour guide, some meals).

Konark Sun Temple – Konark, Odisha

  • Why it's a wonder :13th‑century chariot-shaped temple, intricately carved wheels representing hours of the day.
  • Entry :₹40 (Indians); ₹600 (foreigners)
  • Best time to visit:November–March, open 6 a.m.–8 p.m.
